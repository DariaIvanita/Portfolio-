Hi, I'm Daria ✨

I’m a frontend developer and creative designer based in Calgary with a passion for building digital experiences that feel modern, interactive, and visually meaningful.

My journey started through creativity and design, which naturally led me into frontend development and interactive web experiences. I love combining aesthetics with code to create projects that not only look beautiful but also feel immersive and engaging.

I’ve worked on projects involving UI/UX design, branding, web development, social media campaigns, and 3D experiences using technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Three.js.

Outside of development, I’m inspired by art, fashion, music, storytelling, and digital creativity. I’m always exploring new ways to bring personality and emotion into the experiences I create.
